About Slameg Group

Founded by Dr. Karanja

Slameg Group was established in 2023 with a mission to make a positive impact through charitable work in our community.

We are proud to celebrate our 2-year anniversary on June 28, 2025, marking two years of dedicated service and community support.

Our vision is to create a world where everyone has access to basic needs and opportunities for growth.
